Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 4, 2026

VillageVeggies (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates a waitlist page for people interested in VillageVeggies. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Email address (required) when you join the waitlist.
  • Optional information you provide, such as whether you are a plant shop/nursery or a plant lover, and your city or location.
  • Referral and attribution data (optional), such as UTM parameters indicating how you found our site.

How we use your information

  • To notify you about the VillageVeggies launch, updates, and early access.
  • To understand interest in VillageVeggies by location or channel.

How your information is processed

Waitlist form submissions are processed by Formspree, which collects the information you submit and forwards it to us.

Our website is hosted on Microsoft Azure Static Web Apps, and our website code is stored on GitHub. These providers are used to deliver the website and manage deployments, but they do not receive your waitlist form submission content.

Data retention

We retain waitlist information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, or until you request its deletion.

Your choices

You may request removal from the waitlist at any time by contacting us at: magee.bradley30@gmail.com.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children’s privacy

VillageVeggies is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at magee.bradley30@gmail.com.
